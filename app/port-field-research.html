Ethnographic Study

I spent a semester studying the local improv comedy community. Along with a report on how the groups create funny scenes for their audience, I examined the conversation between audience and actors. The feedback loop is an interesting combination of visual and verbal communication. The actors are adept at reading audience cues to tailor each performance.

Focus Group

In order to research user needs for a city website, I conducted a focus group of residents to determine their needs. The group members were recruited according to four personas.

  • Newcomer requires an area to outline city service schedules in a printable format
  • Retired resident wishes to review the minutes of town meetings the morning after they occur
  • Family resident needs to see Tweets and Facebook messages regarding school closures
  • Casual resident would like to inform the city managers of road problems
