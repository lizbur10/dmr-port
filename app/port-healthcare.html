MedLynk Research

While at Milcare (Herman Miller's healthcare subsidiary), I worked on a team that developed a medication management system for hospitals called MedLynk.

We were alpha-testing a cart-based medication management product and were examining static storage options.

Workflow Diagrams

As part of the MedLynk research, we investigated workflows in various hospital departments. It helped us understand the entire life cycle of patient treatment and materials management with regard to medication administration.

MAR Scanner

During the development process, we created Medication Administration Records (MAR) with barcodes next to each medication name. The nurses used MARs to document patient care. With the addition of a scan at the time of administration, the unit would communicate (via RF) the information to the pharmacy. The scanner sat atop the medication cart. If the medication required was a narcotic, the scan would unlock the narcotic drawer and prompt a count. If there was a problem with the drug or a change in the patient's prescription, the nurse would be notified via the screen on the scanner housing.
